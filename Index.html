Most people fail not because they are not smart enough — they fail because distractions win on bad days. These rules protect you from yourself.
Always do these ✅
📵
Phone in another room — always
Not face-down on desk. In another room. Every notification causes a 23-minute focus interruption.
🚫
Block social media during study hours
Use Cold Turkey or Freedom. Instagram Reels and YouTube Shorts are engineered to steal your attention. Block them.
⌨️
Code first, watch later
Never watch a tutorial without IDE open. Watching without typing is entertainment, not learning.
🎯
One topic at a time — finish before moving on
Jumping ahead = knowing a little about everything, mastering nothing.
📣
Tell someone your daily goal each morning
Text a friend or post in Discord. Public accountability is more powerful than any app.
🔁
Revisit problems — Day 3, Day 7, Day 14
You will forget. That is normal. The fix is spaced repetition. A problem you forgot and re-solved sticks forever.
Never do these ❌
❌
Tutorial hell
After any tutorial: close it and rebuild from scratch. If you cannot, you did not learn it.
❌
Switch frameworks mid-phase
Every switch resets muscle memory and costs 2 weeks. Commit to the plan. No exceptions.
❌
Compare yourself to others online
Compare only to who you were last week. That is the only meaningful metric.
❌
Skip sleep to study more
5 focused hours beats 9 exhausted hours. Sleep consolidates memory. Part of the plan.
❌
Miss 2 days in a row
One missed day fine. Two breaks the habit loop. Never miss twice. No exceptions, no negotiations.
❌
Wait until you feel ready to apply
You will never feel fully ready. Apply from Week 21 regardless.
